What this service covers
Area Efficiency
Full area breakdown — primary rooms, wet areas, closets, circulation, balcony. Usable interior % vs. benchmark. Lost area quantified in m² and AED.
Code Compliance
Every room checked against Dubai Building Code minimums — dimensions, area, and passage clearances. Failures flagged explicitly.
Zoning Analysis
Public/private separation, guest vs. resident circulation, acoustic exposure, arrival sequence, and bedroom distribution logic.
Room-by-Room Audit
Each space compared against DBC minimum and GCC best practice — bed clearances, kitchen passage, living depth, bathroom sizing.
Ranked Weaknesses
All deficiencies ranked by severity — from critical (cannot be fixed by interior design) to marginal (fixable with fit-out). No generic observations.
Professional Verdict
Scored report with final recommendation — whether to proceed, negotiate, or compare alternatives. Fit-out brief priorities included.
